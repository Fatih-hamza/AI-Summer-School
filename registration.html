What's Included

  • Full access to all lectures and workshops
  • Course materials and resources
  • Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and refreshments during the program
  • Certificate of completion
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts
  • Accommodations during the week for all participants

Eligibility

The AI Summer School is open to:

  • Graduate students in computer science, engineering, or related fields
  • Researchers and professionals working in AI and machine learning
  • Industry practitioners interested in foundation models and generative AI
  • Intermediate knowledge of machine learning and Python programming is required

How to Apply

To apply for the AI Summer School:

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit your CV/resume
  3. Provide a brief statement of interest (max 500 words)
  4. For student registration, upload a valid student ID
  5. Pay the registration fee upon acceptance
