Scientific Committee

Meet the distinguished members of our scientific committee who ensure the academic excellence of the AI Summer School.

Jamal BENHAMOU

CEO
Soft Centre

Jamal BENHAMOU has worked for 30 years in the field of restructuring and operational development of business projects, particularly within the IT sector, with a strong predilection for the implementation of incubation and innovation support systems.

His strong sensitivity to disruptive innovation, in multicultural and cross-border project contexts, led him to manage the Soft Center (R&D and software innovation center). And this for 15 years now, during which time he has managed numerous Open Innovation programs combining different practices such as Design Thinking, Lean Startup, Design Sprint and Growth Hacking.
