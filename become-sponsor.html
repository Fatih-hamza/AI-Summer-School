Become a Sponsor

We are currently seeking sponsors and partners for the second edition of the AI Summer School. Your support will help make this event a resounding success, contributing to advancing AI education and empowering the next generation of researchers and innovators in Morocco.

Why Sponsor?

  • Gain visibility among emerging AI talent, researchers, and academic institutions
  • Showcase your commitment through logo placement (website, printed materials, lanyards)
  • Opportunity for speaking or leading a panel session or networking events
  • Associate your brand with gender balance, diversity, and capacity building in AI

Audience & Format

We expect about 50 participants (Master's, PhD students, early-career researchers) from institutions across Morocco. The program combines expert-led lectures, hands-on workshops, and collaborative project ideation and prototyping.

If your organization wishes to explore our sponsorship packages or partnership opportunities, please reach out to:

events-sponsorship@morocco.ai

We are happy to provide full details, and tailor packages to your goals and capacity.
