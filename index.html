Each day is carefully structured to balance theoretical learning with practical application. Mornings are dedicated to foundational concepts presented by expert speakers, while afternoons focus on hands-on implementation and project development.

Evening activities provide valuable opportunities to build connections with fellow participants, mentors, and speakers in more relaxed settings. This comprehensive approach ensures participants gain both technical skills and professional networks.

About the Organizers

The Institut National des Postes et Télécommunications is one of Morocco's premier engineering schools, specializing in telecommunications and information technology education and research.

MoroccoAI is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering the development and adoption of AI in Morocco. It is a collaborative platform bringing together government, industry, academia, and civil society to empower AI Transformation in Morocco and build an innovative and inclusive AI ecosystem.

IMT Nord Europe is a top French engineering school renowned for its excellence in digital sciences, engineering, and innovation, fostering international collaboration and advanced research.

Soft Centre is a software innovation hub established around 2010 that serves as a national innovation catalyst bridging academia, industry, and students to accelerate digital transformation and develop novel IT solutions in Morocco.

This strategic partnership brings together academic excellence and industry connections to create an unparalleled learning experience for participants and strengthen Morocco's AI ecosystem.

Venue

The summer school will take place in the heart of the beautiful city of Rabat, at the Institut National des Postes et Télécommunications (INPT).

Rabat, the capital of Morocco, is located on the Atlantic coast at the mouth of the Bou Regreg River. Founded in the 12th century, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Morocco's four Imperial cities. Recognized by CNN as a top travel destination, Rabat is accessible by train and via Rabat–Salé Airport.
