Technical Curriculum Highlights

Foundations

Core principles and architectures underlying modern AI and machine learning.

Methodologies

State-of-the-art approaches and best practices for developing robust AI systems.

Labs

Hands-on sessions to apply theoretical concepts using real-world datasets and tools.

Research-Industry Fit

Bridging academic innovation with industry needs to drive impactful AI solutions.

Specialized Research Tracks

Advances in Arabic NLP
Cutting-edge research in language technologies for Arabic
ML for Medical Imaging
AI applications transforming healthcare diagnostics
Small Language Modeling
Efficient AI systems for resource-constrained environments

Our specialized tracks offer deep dives into some of the most promising areas of AI research with particular relevance to Morocco and the region. Participants can focus on areas aligned with their interests and career goals, learning from researchers at the cutting edge of these fields.

Each track combines theoretical foundations with practical applications, ensuring participants gain both depth of understanding and hands-on experience with tools and techniques they can immediately apply to their own work.
