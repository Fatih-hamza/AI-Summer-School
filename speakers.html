Meet The Expert Advisers Driving The Event's Agenda

The AI Spring School presents a distinguished lineup of speakers who bridge the gap between academic research and industry innovation. Our carefully selected experts combine theoretical expertise with practical insights, offering participants a comprehensive understanding of AI's current landscape and future directions.

Prof. Alberto Del Bimbo

Professor of Computer Engineering
University of Florence

Prof. Alberto Del Bimbo is a renowned expert in multimedia and computer vision, with decades of experience in academic research and leadership. He has contributed to major advances in video analysis and pattern recognition.

Session: "Advanced Computer Vision: From Theory to Real-World Applications"

Prof. Del Bimbo's session will cover the intersection of multimedia, AI, and computer vision, with a focus on real-world applications and future trends.

Learn more:

Dr. Fahad Shahbaz Khan

AI Professor
Linköping University

Prof. Fahad Shahbaz Khan is a prominent expert in computer vision and deep learning, known for his impactful work on image restoration and enhancement. He has led major advances in transformer-based models for tasks like deblurring, deraining, and denoising.

Session: "Multimodal Foundation Models: Building the Next Generation of AI"

In his session, Dr. Khan will explore the latest advances in multimodal foundation models, discussing practical challenges and opportunities. Attendees will gain insights into model architectures, training strategies, and deployment best practices for large-scale AI systems.

Learn more:

Prof. Mohamed Daoudi

Professor of Computer Vision
IMT Nord Europe

Prof. Mohamed Daoudi is a leading researcher in computer vision and AI, with a focus on 3D shape analysis and facial recognition. He has published extensively and is a frequent speaker at international conferences.

Session: "3D Vision and AI: Breaking New Ground in Shape Analysis"

In his session, Prof. Daoudi will discuss the latest advances in 3D vision and its applications in AI, including practical demonstrations and research insights.

Computer Vision and machine learning
INRIA
