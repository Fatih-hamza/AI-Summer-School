Meet The Expert Advisers Driving The Event's Agenda

At the heart of The AI School Spring lies our distinguished Ambassador Chapter. This esteemed group - featuring some of the brightest minds from the University Trento, central Florida, IMT Nord Europe, INPT, and beyond - are not just advisors; they are the architects of the Summit's cutting-edge event agenda, ensuring that every moment you spend with us is an investment in your future.

Prof. Mohamed Daoudi

Professor of Computer Science
IMT Nord Europe

Prof. Mohamed Daoudi is a leading researcher in computer vision and AI, with a focus on 3D shape analysis and facial recognition. He has published extensively and is a frequent speaker at international conferences.

In his session, Prof. Daoudi will discuss the latest advances in 3D vision and its applications in AI, including practical demonstrations and research insights.

Prof. Alberto Del Bimbo

Professor of Computer Engineering
University of Florence

Prof. Alberto Del Bimbo is a renowned expert in multimedia and computer vision, with decades of experience in academic research and leadership. He has contributed to major advances in video analysis and pattern recognition.

Prof. Del Bimbo's session will cover the intersection of multimedia, AI, and computer vision, with a focus on real-world applications and future trends.

Dr. Jane Smith

AI Research Director
University of Rabat

Dr. Jane Smith is a leading AI researcher with over 15 years of experience in multimodal foundation models. Her work bridges academia and industry, focusing on the development of robust, scalable AI systems for real-world applications. She is passionate about mentoring young researchers and advancing the field of responsible AI.

In her session, Dr. Smith will explore the latest advances in multimodal foundation models, discussing practical challenges and opportunities. Attendees will gain insights into model architectures, training strategies, and deployment best practices for large-scale AI systems.

Prof. John Doe

Machine Learning Professor
MoroccoAI

Prof. John Doe is a renowned professor at MoroccoAI, specializing in generative AI and deep learning. He has published extensively in top-tier conferences and is known for his engaging teaching style and commitment to open science.

Prof. Doe's session will cover the latest trends in generative AI, including hands-on demonstrations of deep learning architectures and their applications in creative industries.
